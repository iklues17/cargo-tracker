My Grantable Authorities



Manage sub authorities under your authorities as followed instructions.

You can make sub roles of each role you have. and role name format must be

'role name you have + underscore + capital letters'


If your role name is 'ROLE_YOU_HAVE',  you can make the sub roles as follows.

        'ROLE_YOU_HAVE_SUB'
        'ROLE_YOU_HAVE_SUB_ROLE'
        'ROLE_YOU_HAVE_SUB_ROLE_NAME'
